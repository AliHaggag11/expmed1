ExpertsMed: Your growth partners in geographic expansion into Emerging Markets. Our expertise meets all your Emerging Markets needs.

ExpertsMed mission is to enhance and support both multinational pharmaceutical/medical companies and distributors as they grow their businesses. We are your partner to penetrate 100+ emerging and developed markets, we are committed to support and develop your markets in order to achieve profitable, sustainable growth.

Emerging markets is a key specialty for ExpertsMed. Emerging markets are dynamic, with rapidly changing environments which require deep geopolitical knowledge and business agility. Geographic expansion is an expensive undertaking for any business, which gets more complicated to achieve with lack of funds and experienced resources. ExpertsMed enables multinationals and distributors to expand their business in an efficient and cost-effective manner. Our clients achieve optimal results with our support on commercial operations, market access, regulatory, compliance, quality, supply chain, operations and marketing. ExpertsMed helps you to operate efficiently, effectively, ethically, and profitably in dynamic, growth markets.

ExpertsMed is headquartered in Dubai and operates under UAE law, which offers a robust framework for corporate activity, governed by comprehensive federal legislation. ExpertsMed consists of experienced professionals with a superior track record not only in commercial, but across the spectrum of business disciplines, supporting multinationals and distributors as they penetrate and expand into new business/markets, with experts on regulatory, medical, compliance, training and education, legal, marketing, supply chain, finance and a variety of other functions.

We are also your partner for developing and enhancing your team’s skills and capabilities via our leadership, development, and mentoring experts. We have an extended team in key locations across the globe, currently including Cairo, London, and Zurich, with Riyadh and Johannesburg to be included in the coming months.
